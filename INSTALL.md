# Install Z1 INSIGHTS on your phone

Z1 INSIGHTS is a Progressive Web App — no App Store, no Play Store, no
download. Open the site in your phone's browser and add it to your home
screen. After install it launches full-screen with its own icon, exactly
like a native app.

Live URL: **https://mr05xau.co.uk**

## iPhone / iPad (Safari)

1. Open `https://mr05xau.co.uk` in **Safari** (not Chrome — iOS only allows
   installs from Safari).
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with an arrow at the bottom of the screen).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Confirm the name "Z1 INSIGHTS" and tap **Add**.
5. The icon now appears on your home screen. Launch it like any other app.

## Android (Chrome, Edge, Brave, Samsung Internet)

1. Open `https://mr05xau.co.uk` in Chrome (or any Chromium browser).
2. You'll usually see an **Install app** banner near the URL bar — tap it.
3. If you don't see the banner: tap the **⋮** menu in the top-right →
   **Install app** (or **Add to Home screen**).
4. Confirm and the icon installs to your launcher.

## Desktop (Chrome, Edge, Brave)

1. Open `https://mr05xau.co.uk`.
2. Click the **install icon** in the address bar (a small monitor with a
   down-arrow), or open the **⋮** menu → **Install Z1 INSIGHTS**.
3. The app opens in its own window and gets a launcher/dock icon.

## What you get when installed

- Full-screen launch (no browser chrome).
- Dark status bar matching the app theme.
- Home-screen icon.
- Faster cold-start than the browser tab.
- All features work: book, AI tutor, quizzes, notebook, journal, paywall.

## Payments

Lifetime access is purchased on the website via Stripe checkout — this
works inside the installed app exactly as it does in the browser. There
is no Apple/Google billing layer and no platform fee.

## Updates

The app updates automatically every time you open it (the latest version
is fetched in the background). No manual update step needed.

## Uninstalling

- **iPhone**: long-press the icon → **Remove App** → **Delete App**.
- **Android**: long-press the icon → drag to **Uninstall**, or Settings →
  Apps → Z1 INSIGHTS → Uninstall.
- **Desktop**: open the app → **⋮** menu → **Uninstall Z1 INSIGHTS**.
